# Important Questions for End-Sem 2025 Examination

This document contains a list of important questions for the End-Semester 2025 Examination, categorized by unit. These questions have been compiled after a deep analysis of the syllabus and previous year question papers.

---

## UNIT 1: Introduction to Storage Technology

1.  **What do you understand by Information Lifecycle Management (ILM)?** Explain its significance in information management and discuss the key strategies for its implementation.
2.  **Explain the evolution of storage technologies.** Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of each phase, from magnetic tapes to cloud storage.
3.  **What is data categorization?** Why is it necessary? Discuss the challenges of managing large volumes of unstructured data and the strategies to overcome them.
4.  **Define a data center.** What are the key requirements for data center elements?
5.  **Differentiate between structured and unstructured data.**

---

## UNIT 2: Storage Systems Architecture

1.  **Explain different RAID levels (0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5) in detail.** Compare them in terms of storage efficiency, cost, read/write performance, and data redundancy. Justify which level is most suitable for specific use cases.
2.  **What is an intelligent disk subsystem?** Explain its key components and how it uses caching to enhance performance.
3.  **Compare and contrast integrated arrays with modular arrays.**
4.  **Explain the physical structure of a disk**, including components like platter, spindle, track, sector, and cylinder.
5.  **What is hot sparing in a RAID configuration?**

---

## UNIT 3: Introduction to Networked Storage

1.  **Compare and contrast DAS, NAS, SAN, and CAS** based on their architecture, connectivity, management, security, and typical use cases.
2.  **Explain the evolution of networked storage architectures** from JBOD to DAS, NAS, SAN, and CAS.
3.  **What are the key elements and components of a Storage Area Network (SAN)?** Analyze the scalability options available for SAN.
4.  **Discuss the benefits of using NAS for file sharing** in a collaborative environment.
5.  **Explain how connectivity standards like SCSI, iSCSI, and Fibre Channel are used** in implementing networked storage solutions.

---

## UNIT 4: Hybrid Storage solutions; Virtualization

1.  **Define Virtualization and discuss its various types:** Memory, Network, Server, Storage, and Appliance Virtualization, with suitable examples.
2.  **Explain the principles of Backup and Disaster Recovery.** What is the significance of RTO (Recovery Time Objective) and RPO (Recovery Point Objective)?
3.  **Discuss the role of industry management standards such as SNMP, SMI-S, and CIM** in managing and monitoring enterprise storage systems.
4.  **What are the key concepts and requirements of a Data Center?** How do power, cooling, scalability, and security influence data center design?
5.  **Critically evaluate the methods used for data tiering in hybrid storage solutions.**

---

## UNIT 5: Information storage on cloud

1.  **Define cloud computing and explain its key characteristics.** Discuss the benefits of using cloud storage over traditional storage methods.
2.  **Explain the different Cloud Service Models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and Deployment Models (Public, Private, Hybrid, Community Cloud)** with suitable examples.
3.  **Describe the architectural framework of cloud computing** and explain the roles of different layers in providing cloud services.
4.  **Discuss the major cloud security and integration challenges.**
5.  **Differentiate between block storage, file storage, and object storage in the cloud.**
